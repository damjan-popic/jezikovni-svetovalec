# Velika začetnica pri naselbinskih imenih

## Practical rule

In Slovenian settlement names (**naselbinska imena**), write all components with an initial capital, except non-initial prepositions and the common nouns **mesto**, **trg**, **vas/vesca**, **selo/sela/selce**, and **naselje** when they are not the first word of the name.

So the model answer for the common student example is: **Novo mesto**, not **Novo Mesto**.

## Source basis

SP 2001 distinguishes settlement and non-settlement geographical names, then states the capitalization rule for settlement names and lists the exceptions above. It gives examples including **Novo mesto**, **Stari trg**, **Dolenja vas**, **Opatje selo**, **Uršna sela**, **Dolenje selce**, and **Ribiško naselje**. Source: SP 2001, PDF page 13, `data/corpus/pages/sp2001/page_0013.md`.

SP 2001 also notes related details such as treatment of non-settlement modifiers and foreign settlement-name elements. Source: SP 2001, PDF page 14, `data/corpus/pages/sp2001/page_0014.md`.
