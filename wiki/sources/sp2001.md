# SP 2001

**Role in this repo:** primary source for normative orthographic answers.

Use this source first for:

- writing sounds and letters,
- capitalization,
- loanword writing,
- punctuation,
- writing together or apart,
- hyphenation and word division,
- abbreviations, symbols, and written signs.

SP 2001 states that pravopis covers zapisovanje glasov, rabo velikih in malih črk, pisanje prevzetih besed, rabo ločil, pisanje skupaj ali narazen, and deljenje. Source: SP 2001, PDF page 9, `data/corpus/pages/sp2001/page_0009.md`.
