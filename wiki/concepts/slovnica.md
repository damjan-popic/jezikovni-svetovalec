# Slovnica

In this repo, **slovnica** is the system-level explanation of Slovenian: sounds, words, word formation, forms, syntax, varieties, communication, and historical development.

Toporišič's table of contents organizes the book around zvrstnost, glasoslovje, besedoslovje, besedotvorje, oblikoslovje, skladnja, sporočanje, and language in time. Source: Toporišič, Slovenska slovnica, PDF pages 12–13, `data/corpus/pages/toporisic_slovnica/page_0012.md`, `data/corpus/pages/toporisic_slovnica/page_0013.md`.

## Use in answers

When a student asks “zakaj”, “katera besedna vrsta”, “kakšna skladenjska vloga”, “kako se sklanja/sprega”, or “katera zvrst”, route to Toporišič first.
