# Pravopis

In this repo, **pravopis** means the rule system for written Slovenian.

SP 2001 explicitly frames pravopis as covering:

- writing sounds,
- upper- and lowercase letters,
- loanword writing,
- punctuation,
- writing together or apart,
- word division/hyphenation.

Source: SP 2001, PDF page 9, `data/corpus/pages/sp2001/page_0009.md`.

## Use in answers

When a student asks “kako se piše”, “velika ali mala začetnica”, “vejica”, “skupaj ali narazen”, or “deljenje”, route to SP 2001 first.
